Pi Analytics design proofs

AI-native data investigation workbench

An analyst receives a large unknown folder, opens Pi Analytics, and asks for a summary. The product reads the material, explains what is inside, maps relationships, helps investigate with questions, tables, charts, notes, and saved findings, then produces a hosted report or presentation.

Product story

Start with a folder. Understand the contents in business language. Investigate financial crime, cost, ROI, or marketing performance. Keep the analyst and AI working together until the conclusion is ready to share.

1. Intake

Drop a folder or choose a data room. The AI begins reading and shows freshness, progress, and initial coverage.

2. Understand

Inventory cards describe files, tables, documents, and business meaning. A relationship diagram shows how entities connect.

3. Investigate

Ask natural-language questions, switch investigation type, inspect evidence, adjust filters, make notes, and save findings.

4. Present

Build a polished report or presentation from saved findings, preview it, and host a shareable version in one click.
